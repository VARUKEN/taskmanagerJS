Neue Aufgabe

Simple task manager with localstorage support.
  • the task lists are draggable
  • the tasks themselves can be dragged to other lists
  • a task can be deleted by clicking on the "minus" icon or be dropped into the trashcan
  • data is stored in the local storage
  • wallpapers can be dynamically swapped
  • used H5BP , Bootstrap , Jquery , JqueryUI
  • sadly its not mobile friendly ( yet )
  • not very optimized , still a lot to do :(
